New Manufacturing Facility Opens in Shanghai

上海新制造工厂正式启用

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. is proud to announce the grand opening of our new manufacturing facility in Shanghai, representing a $50 million investment in cutting-edge production technology.

The facility spans 100,000 square meters and features fully automated production lines for calcium carbide and cyanamide derivatives.

This expansion increases our total production capacity by 40% and creates 200 new jobs in the region.

The facility incorporates the latest environmental protection systems, including closed-loop water recycling and solar power generation.

ABC化工有限公司自豪地宣布位于上海的新制造工厂正式开业，这代表着5000万美元的尖端生产技术投资。

该工厂占地10万平方米，配备全自动电石和氰胺衍生物生产线。

此次扩建使我们的总产能增加40%，并在该地区创造200个新就业岗位。

工厂采用最新环保系统，包括闭环水循环和太阳能发电。
