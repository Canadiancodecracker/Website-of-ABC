Festival Greetings & Employee Care

As the 2026 Year of the Horse Spring Festival approached, ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. organized a series of heartwarming Chinese New Year activities. These events were designed to foster a warm and harmonious festive atmosphere, allowing every employee to feel the company's sincere care and the warmth of being part of one big family.

Spring Festival Gift Packages

Prior to the festival, the company prepared practical and auspiciously symbolic Spring Festival gift packages for all employees. The distribution was carried out in an orderly manner, with employees beaming with smiles. A long-serving employee shared: "The company's welfare benefits make us feel our value and appreciation."

Festive Food & Celebrations

To celebrate the joyous occasion, the company also arranged a delicious festive meal for employees, featuring an array of carefully prepared dishes. The spread brought colleagues together in a spirit of warmth and togetherness, making the holiday season even more enjoyable and festive.

Leadership Visits Frontline Workers

On the first day of the 2026 Chinese New Year (Chinese New Year's Day), the General Manager personally led a team to visit various factory areas and workshops, extending sincere greetings to frontline workers. The employees remained committed to their posts during the holiday. The leadership repeatedly reminded workers to arrange their work and rest schedules appropriately and to always ensure safe production.

These activities reflect ABC Chemical's commitment to employee well-being and its spirit of unity as one family. The company wishes all employees a happy, healthy, and prosperous Year of the Horse!
