ABC Chemical to Host Global Distributor Conference in Q1 2025

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. announced today it will hold its biennial Global Distributor Conference in Singapore in February 2025. The event will bring together key distribution partners, logistics experts, and regional sales leaders from over 30 countries. The focus of the four-day event will be on unveiling the Q2 product roadmap, optimizing inventory management, and strengthening logistics chains to ensure consistent supply of calcium and cyanamide solutions worldwide.
