ABC Chemical Unveils 'Super Green' Initiative: Targeting 20% Reduction in Carbon Footprint by 2026

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. today announced its most significant corporate commitment to environmental responsibility with the launch of the 'Super Green' Initiative. This comprehensive, company-wide program is strategically designed to achieve a minimum 20% reduction in Scope 1 and Scope 2 carbon emissions by the end of 2026, using 2024 as the baseline.

Key investments under this initiative include the installation of new, advanced closed-loop filtration systems at our primary manufacturing plants and a commitment to a 100% switch to renewable energy sources for all corporate offices and R&D facilities by the end of next year. This initiative underscores our core philosophy: Chemistry You Can Rely On, for the present and the future.
