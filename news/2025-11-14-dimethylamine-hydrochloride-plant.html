ABC Chemical Launches State-of-the-Art API Project: Dimethylamine Hydrochloride Plant in Ningxia

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. has initiated a major project in Ningxia for the production of Dimethylamine Hydrochloride (DMH) API, with the first phase targeting a capacity of 10,000 MT/Year.

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. is proud to announce the commencement of a new, strategic production project at its facility in Ningxia, China. This initiative focuses on the manufacturing of the high-demand Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API), Dimethylamine Hydrochloride (DMH). The project is being executed in phases, with the first phase designed to achieve an annual production capacity of 10,000 metric tons (MT/Year).

The plant will utilize state-of-the-art technology and proprietary processes to ensure the highest standards of purity and efficiency required for pharmaceutical-grade materials. The production line is currently under construction and is scheduled to be fully commissioned and operational by March 2026.

This expansion marks a major step in ABC Chemical's strategy to diversify its product portfolio and strengthen its position in the global pharmaceutical supply chain.
