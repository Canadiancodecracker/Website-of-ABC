Dicyandiamide (DCD)

C₂H₄N₄
CAS: 461-58-5

General Information

Chemical Name
Dicyandiamide
Synonyms
Cyanoguanidine, Dicyanamide
Molecular Weight
84.08
Appearance
White crystalline solid

Physical Properties

Relative Density 1.40 (25 °C)
Melting Point 209–212 °C
Solubility Soluble in ethanol; slightly soluble in cold water; more soluble in hot water
Stability Stable under dry conditions; non-combustible

Technical Specifications

Item Specification
Appearance White crystalline powder
Purity (Dicyandiamide) ≥ 99.5%
Moisture ≤ 0.30%
Ash Content ≤ 0.05%
Calcium Content ≤ 200 ppm
Melting Point 209–212 °C

Structural Formula

H₂N-C(=NH)-NH-C≡N

Applications

Used as a curing agent for epoxy resins, in the production of slow-release fertilizers, and as an intermediate for pharmaceuticals and dye fixing agents.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from incompatible substances. Keep containers tightly closed. Protect from moisture.

Packaging

25 kg net plastic woven bags with polyethylene liner.
