Dicyandiamide (DCD)

Dicyandiamide (DCD) is a white crystalline powder with high purity and consistent reactivity. It is sparingly soluble in cold water, more soluble in hot water, and soluble in ethanol. DCD remains stable under dry conditions, non-flammable, and exhibits high chemical stability.
