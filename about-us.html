About Us

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. — Advanced Calcium & Cyanamide Solutions

ABC Chemical Co., Ltd. is a global manufacturer specializing in high-purity calcium cyanamide, dicyandiamide (DCD), cyanamide derivatives, creatine series products, and fine nitrogen-carbon materials.

Built on technological innovation and strict quality governance, ABC Chemical combines large-scale production with international quality standards, ensuring consistency, traceability, and safety across every batch.

We operate modern manufacturing facilities equipped with advanced reactors, automated systems, continuous monitoring instruments, and in-house laboratories for analytical testing.

At ABC Chemical, we believe chemistry should create value responsibly. Our teams integrate R&D innovation, sustainable manufacturing, and long-term customer partnerships.

  • ISO-certified quality, environment, and safety systems
  • Global supply chain and 120+ distribution partners
