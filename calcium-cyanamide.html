Product Description

Calcium cyanamide is a slow-release nitrogen fertilizer. It is used to increase crop yield and effectively kill harmful bacteria and insects, optimize soil structure, eliminate soil hardening, alleviate soil acidification, and supplement soil with calcium and nitrogen.

Applications

A multifunctional agrochemical and chemical intermediate, enabling soil improvement, nitrogen fertilization, and specialty NCN syntheses.

  • Slow-release nitrogen fertilizer for increased crop yield
  • Kill harmful bacteria and insects in soil
  • Optimize soil structure and eliminate soil hardening
  • Alleviate soil acidification
  • Supplement soil with calcium and nitrogen
  • Intermediate for dicyandiamide production
  • Steel desulfurization

Storage & Safety

Storage:

Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from moisture and heat. Keep container tightly closed.

Safety Notes:

Avoid contact with skin and eyes. Use in well-ventilated areas. Wear appropriate PPE when handling.
