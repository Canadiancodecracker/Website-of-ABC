Key Advantages

Stable acetylene yield

Optimized grain size and controlled CaC₂ purity deliver predictable gas output for cutting, welding, heating, and synthesis applications.

Engineered for industrial durability

Designed to withstand varying humidity and transportation conditions with reduced powdering and minimized degradation.

Low impurity profile

Strict QC minimizes phosphorus, sulfur, and metallic residues to protect downstream equipment and improve combustion behavior.

Efficient material handling

Granule uniformity improves feed efficiency in automated acetylene generators and reduces equipment wear.

  • Oxy-acetylene metal cutting & welding
