Applications

High‑purity feedstock supporting acetylene generation and downstream specialty syntheses.

Storage & Safety

Storage:

Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from moisture. Keep container tightly closed.

Safety Notes:

Reacts violently with water to produce flammable acetylene gas. Handle with care. Use appropriate PPE.
